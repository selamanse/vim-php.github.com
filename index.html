Mission

We are a group of PHP developers with use VIM every day or not.

As a first step we would we'd like other phpVimmers for share: trips, plugins, experiens and all other breakages caused by VIM.

In addition to that vim-php is a Github organization that collects a lot of community shared plugins about Vim and PHP.
Have you a plugin? You can share it with this organization you will be a first maintainers but you can take advantage of the community's aid.
